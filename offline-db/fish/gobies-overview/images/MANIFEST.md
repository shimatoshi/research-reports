## Taxonomic Notes

Several Wikimedia Commons filenames use Japanese common names inconsistent with their binomial labels — this reflects the ongoing Rhinogobius taxonomic revision. Specifically:

- `File:Rhinogobius_nagoyae_Nakagawa.webp` caption says シマヨシノボリ (R. brunneus sensu lato)
- `File:Rhinogobius_fluviatilis.webp` caption says オオヨシノボリ (usually R. nagoyae)
- `File:Rhinogobius_kurodai_*.jpg` captions say トウヨシノボリ (often attributed to R. kurodai but also used for undescribed types)

These discrepancies are common in aquarium/field photos uploaded before the 2010s taxonomic reshuffle. Caption-based classification in this dataset should be treated as tentative.
